## Methodology of the Road to Autonomy Index
The Road to Autonomy Index is a benchmarking system that evaluates and ranks autonomous vehicle companies based on various parameters such as operations, scale, revenue, commercial partnerships, manufacturing, and safety record. The system utilizes a generative AI platform to search relevant global public databases, including federal and state reports, SEC documents, public exchanges, and other data. This data is then weighted and analyzed to provide a comprehensive ranking of the companies.

The index takes a global approach, which means that it considers companies from all over the world, not just those based in the United States. This provides a more nuanced and accurate picture of the autonomous vehicle landscape. The index is updated every 12 hours, ensuring that the rankings are always current and reflective of the latest developments in the industry.

### Key Parameters of the Road to Autonomy Index
The Road to Autonomy Index considers the following key parameters when evaluating and ranking autonomous vehicle companies:
* Operations: This includes the number of vehicles on the road, the number of miles driven, and the complexity of the routes being navigated.
* Scale: This includes the size of the company, the number of employees, and the scope of its operations.
* Revenue: This includes the revenue generated by the company, the growth rate of its revenue, and the diversity of its revenue streams.
* Commercial partnerships: This includes the number and quality of partnerships the company has with other businesses, the scope of these partnerships, and the revenue generated by these partnerships.
* Manufacturing: This includes the company's manufacturing capabilities, the quality of its vehicles, and the efficiency of its production processes.
* Safety record: This includes the company's safety record, the number of accidents it has been involved in, and the measures it has taken to improve safety.
