## Introduction to Media Centers
The world of media centers has evolved significantly over the years, with various platforms emerging to cater to different user needs. Two popular options in this space are Plex and Jellyfin. While Plex has been a household name for a while, its recent design choices have left some users disappointed, prompting them to explore alternatives like Jellyfin.

## Plex: A Overview
Plex is a well-known media center platform that offers a range of features, including live TV and DVR functionality, remote access, and a social platform for users to share and discover content. However, the author's dissatisfaction with Plex's recent design choices, which prioritize ad-supported streaming over personal media collection and DVR functionality, has led to a search for alternative solutions. For instance, the annual subscription fee of $70 and the lifetime pass, which was initially $250 but later increased to $750, may not be justifiable for users who primarily use Plex for personal media collection.

## Jellyfin: A Potential Replacement
Jellyfin, on the other hand, is a free and open-source application that offers a range of features, including media scanning, local network access, and clients for major desktop, mobile, and smart TV platforms. It also has a plug-in repository and supports third-party plug-ins, making it a highly customizable option. As mentioned by the author, "If you're the kind of person who enjoys endlessly tweaking things, Jellyfin is a dream." Furthermore, Jellyfin's support for IPTV and its workaround for live TV using Next PVR make it an attractive alternative to Plex. ### Key Features of Jellyfin
Some of the key features of Jellyfin include:
* Free and open-source application
* Server download and media scanning
* Local network access and clients for major platforms
* Plug-in repository and support for third-party plug-ins
* Customizable web interface
* IPTV support and workaround for live TV using Next PVR
* Electronic program guide data available through Schedules Direct for $35/year
