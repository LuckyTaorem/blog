Gaming laptops have become an essential part of the gaming industry, offering players a portable and powerful way to play their favorite games. However, the recent rise in costs of RAM and storage has made gaming laptops too expensive for many gamers. In this article, we will explore the reasons behind the high cost of gaming laptops and discuss the MSI Katana laptop, which offers a good value for its price.

### The Rise of Gaming Laptop Costs
The cost of gaming laptops has increased significantly in recent years, making it difficult for many gamers to afford a high-performance laptop. The main reason for this increase is the rising cost of RAM and storage. As games become more demanding, laptops require more powerful hardware to run them smoothly, which drives up the cost. Additionally, the shortage of certain components, such as GPUs and CPUs, has also contributed to the price increase.
