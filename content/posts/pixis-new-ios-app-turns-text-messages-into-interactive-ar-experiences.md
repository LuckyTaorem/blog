## What is Interactive Augmented Reality?
Before diving into Pixi's app, it's essential to understand what interactive augmented reality entails. Augmented reality (AR) is a technology that overlays digital information and images onto the real world, using the camera on a device. Interactive AR takes this a step further by allowing users to engage with these digital overlays in real-time. This can include playing games, solving puzzles, or participating in other immersive activities that blur the line between the physical and digital worlds.
