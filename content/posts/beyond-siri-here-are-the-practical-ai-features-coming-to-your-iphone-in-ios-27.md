## Bill Splitting with Apple Cash
One of the most exciting features coming to iOS 27 is the ability to split restaurant bills using Apple Cash. This feature, powered by Apple Intelligence, allows users to take a photo of the receipt or upload a photo, and then select the items they ordered. The feature will then calculate the total cost, including tax and tip, and allow users to request payment from others in the group chat. This feature is not only convenient but also eliminates the need for manual calculations and awkward conversations about who owes what.

The process of using this feature is straightforward:
* Take a photo of the receipt or upload a photo
* Select the items you ordered from the receipt
* Share a request for others to do the same by messaging the group chat
* Others can select their items and quantities, even selecting a half (1/2) if they split with another
* To pay, double-click just as you would for any other Apple Cash transaction

This feature is a great example of how AI can be used to simplify everyday tasks and make life easier for users. By leveraging Apple Intelligence, the feature can accurately extract key details from the bill, such as the items ordered, quantities, tip, and total.

## Password Security with AI
Another significant feature coming to iOS 27 is the password-updating feature, which leverages AI to identify weak and compromised passwords. This feature will securely navigate websites, signing in and upgrading passwords to new, more secure versions. This is a major step forward in password security, as it eliminates the need for manual password updates and reduces the risk of passwords being compromised.

The benefits of this feature are numerous:
* Automatically identifies weak and compromised passwords
* Securely navigates websites to update passwords
* Eliminates the need for manual password updates
* Reduces the risk of passwords being compromised

This feature is particularly important in today's digital landscape, where data breaches are becoming increasingly common. By using AI to identify and update weak passwords, Apple is taking a proactive approach to password security and helping to protect its users from potential threats.
