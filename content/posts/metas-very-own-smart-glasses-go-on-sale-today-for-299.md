## Introduction to Meta Smart Glasses
Meta, a leading technology company, has recently launched its new line of smart glasses, marking a significant step in the evolution of wearable technology. According to Peter Bristol, Meta's vice president of industrial design, the goal is to make smart glasses as accessible and convenient as public transportation. The company aims to achieve this by making its smart glasses more affordable, customizable, and comfortable to wear. In this article, we will delve into the details of Meta's new smart glasses, exploring their features, design, and pricing.

## Key Features of Meta Smart Glasses
The new Meta smart glasses come with several key features that set them apart from their predecessors. Some of the notable features include:
* Adjustable nose pads that can be tilted in three directions to fit the bridge of the nose
* Adjustable temple tips that can be tilted in or out to suit different face shapes
* Overextension hinges that allow the arms of the glasses to flare out slightly, making it easier for people with wider heads to find a good fit
* A range of customization options, including tinted lenses and various frame colors
* Support for prescription lenses, with a power range of -12 to +2.25

### Meta Adventurer and Meta Fury
The Meta Adventurer and Meta Fury are two of the three new smart glasses models launched by Meta. Both models have a classic rectangular shape, with the Meta Adventurer coming in standard and large sizes. The Meta Fury has a similar design but with a less boxy shape. Both models feature 26 customization options, including tinted lenses and various frame colors such as Racing Green and Sandstone.

### Meta Starfire
The Meta Starfire is a special edition smart glass designed in collaboration with Kylie Jenner. This model has a unique design, with a tiny gemstone on the lens and a metal nose pad that prevents makeup from being absorbed. The Meta AI voice assistant on the Starfire edition also features an AI-generated version of Jenner's voice, complete with custom sounds unique to this model. The case of the Starfire edition includes a note from Jenner and has a built-in mirror. The Meta Starfire is priced at $399, which is slightly higher than the other two models.

## Pricing and Availability
The new Meta smart glasses are priced starting at $299, which is significantly lower than the Ray-Ban Gen 2 glasses launched by Meta last year. The lower price point is due to the absence of luxury branding, such as Ray-Ban or Oakley, which allows Meta to keep costs down. The Meta smart glasses are available for purchase from glasses shops like Lens Crafters, with or without a prescription.

## Partnership with Essilor Luxottica
Meta has partnered with Essilor Luxottica, a leading eyewear conglomerate, to manufacture and distribute the new smart glasses. This partnership allows Meta to leverage Essilor Luxottica's expertise in eyewear manufacturing and distribution, ensuring that the smart glasses are of high quality and widely available.

## Competition in the Smart Glasses Market
The smart glasses market is becoming increasingly competitive, with several companies launching their own versions of smart glasses. Recently, Snap launched its AR Specs, which were criticized for being bulky and expensive. Meta's chief technology officer, Andrew Bosworth, commented that competition is good for customers and for Meta, as it allows the company to learn from different design choices and see how people respond to them.

## Design and Comfort
Design and comfort are crucial components of smart glasses. Meta has focused on making its smart glasses more comfortable to wear by introducing features such as adjustable nose pads and temple tips. The company has also worked on reducing the weight and size of the glasses, making them more convenient to wear for extended periods.

### Comparison with Snap AR Specs
The Meta smart glasses are significantly more compact and lightweight than the Snap AR Specs. While the Snap AR Specs are more powerful and capable, their bulky design and high price point have been criticized by many. In contrast, the Meta smart glasses offer a more balanced approach, with a focus on design, comfort, and affordability.

## Technical Specifications
The technical specifications of the Meta smart glasses are not fully disclosed, but it is expected that they will have similar features to other smart glasses on the market. Some of the expected features include:
* A high-resolution display
* A powerful processor
* A long-lasting battery
* Support for augmented reality (AR) experiences
* Integration with Meta's AI voice assistant

## Conclusion
The launch of Meta's new smart glasses marks a significant step in the evolution of wearable technology. With a focus on design, comfort, and affordability, Meta is poised to make smart glasses more accessible to a wider audience. The partnership with Essilor Luxottica and the absence of luxury branding have allowed Meta to keep costs down, making the smart glasses more competitive in the market. As the smart glasses market continues to grow and evolve, it will be interesting to see how Meta's new smart glasses perform and how they compare to other products on the market.
