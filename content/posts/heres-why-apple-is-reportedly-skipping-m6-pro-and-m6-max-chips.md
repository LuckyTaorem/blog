## Introduction to Apple's Chip Development
Apple is reportedly skipping the M6 Pro and M6 Max chips, according to Mark Gurman from Bloomberg. This decision is part of Apple's strategy to focus on the next generation of chips, the M7 series. The M7 series is expected to bring significant improvements in neural-processing capabilities, which will be crucial for Apple's future products.

## The Importance of Neural-Processing Upgrades
As stated by Mark Gurman, "Apple had been planning major neural-processing upgrades for the M7 family and ultimately decided those improvements were important enough to justify accelerating the next generation rather than completing the M6 lineup." This indicates that Apple is prioritizing the development of the M7 series, which will likely power future Apple devices, including Macs and iOS devices.
