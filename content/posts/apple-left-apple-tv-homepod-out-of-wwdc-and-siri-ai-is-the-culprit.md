## The Significance of Apple TV and HomePod

Apple TV is a digital media player that allows users to access various streaming services, play games, and control other smart devices in their homes. HomePod, on the other hand, is a smart speaker that utilizes Siri AI to play music, answer questions, and control other smart devices. Both products have received updates and improvements over the years, but their absence from WWDC 2026 raises questions about Apple's plans for these devices.
