## Introduction to Robotic Pool Cleaners
Robotic pool cleaners have revolutionized the way we maintain our swimming pools. These devices are designed to automatically clean the pool floor, walls, and waterline, saving us time and effort. With so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one. In this article, we will explore the best robotic pool cleaners of 2026, highlighting their features, specifications, and prices.

## Top Robotic Pool Cleaners of 2026
The following are some of the top robotic pool cleaners of 2026:
* Beatbot Aqua Sense 2 Ultra: This high-end model features near-perfect cleaning capabilities, a powerful battery with six hours of charge, AI-powered debris detection, and a solid mobile app.
* iGarden M1-AI series: This series offers advanced navigation, wireless charging, and a compact design.
* Dreame robotic pool cleaners: These cleaners are known for their affordability and ease of use.
