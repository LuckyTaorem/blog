## Technical Deep Dive  

### AirPods Pro 3  

The third generation of AirPods Pro introduces Apple‑custom drivers, an adaptive EQ, and a new H2 chip that promises lower latency and improved battery life. The $50 discount translates to roughly a 12% price reduction, which is significant given the premium positioning of the line. Key technical specs include:

- **Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)** with a new outward‑facing microphone for better wind resistance.  
- **Spatial Audio** with dynamic head tracking, now supported on more third‑party apps.  
- **Battery Life**: Up to 6 hours of listening time on a single charge, plus 30 hours with the charging case.  

These improvements reinforce Apple’s dominance in the true‑wireless market and set a high bar for competitors.

AirTag 2 refines the original’s precision‑finding capabilities by integrating a U1 chip with enhanced Ultra‑Wideband (UWB) accuracy.
