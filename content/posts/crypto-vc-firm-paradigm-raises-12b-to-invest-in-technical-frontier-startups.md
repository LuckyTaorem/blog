## Introduction to Paradigm and Its Investment Strategy
Paradigm, a crypto VC firm, has recently raised $1.2 billion for its third venture fund (Fund III) to invest in "technical frontier" startups. This move marks a significant expansion of the firm's investment scope, beyond cryptocurrency investments to include robotics and AI. Founded in 2018 by Matt Huang and Fred Ehrsam, Paradigm has already made investments in notable companies such as drone delivery company Zipline and space startup True Anomaly.

### Background on Paradigm's Founders
Matt Huang and Fred Ehrsam, the co-founders of Paradigm, bring a wealth of experience in the tech and finance industries. Fred Ehrsam, a former partner at Sequoia, has a deep understanding of the startup ecosystem and the requirements for successful investments. Matt Huang, with his background in technology and entrepreneurship, complements Ehrsam's expertise, enabling Paradigm to make informed investment decisions.

## Investment Focus and Strategy
Paradigm's investment strategy is focused on identifying and supporting startups that are pushing the boundaries of technology. The firm's managing partner, Alana Palmedo, emphasized the importance of exploring new areas of investment, stating, "There's so much else happening right now that's pretty hard to ignore." This approach has led Paradigm to invest in a diverse range of companies, including those in the crypto, blockchain, and AI spaces.

### Notable Investments and Partnerships
Some of Paradigm's notable investments include:
* Zipline: A drone delivery company that has partnered with governments and companies to deliver medical supplies and other essential items.
* True Anomaly: A space startup that is developing innovative technologies for space exploration and satellite communications.
* Foundry: A blockchain and crypto-focused company that is developing infrastructure for the crypto industry.
* Centaur: An agent tools company that is creating solutions for the growing demand for autonomous systems.
