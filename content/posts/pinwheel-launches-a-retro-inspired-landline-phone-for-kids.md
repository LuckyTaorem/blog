## Introduction to Pinwheel Home
Pinwheel, a kid-focused tech company, has launched a retro-inspired landline phone for kids called Pinwheel Home. This innovative device is designed to provide a safe and fun way for children to communicate with their loved ones. In this article, we will delve into the features and specifications of the Pinwheel Home phone and explore its potential impact on the tech industry.

## Features and Specifications
The Pinwheel Home phone comes in two models: Spark and Classic. The Spark model starts at $68 and is available in four colors: white, black, blue, and purple. The Classic model costs $79 and includes a retro-style handset and customizable stickers. It is available in three colors: pink, black, and white. Both models feature speed dial, voicemail, and future updates will introduce three-way calling. The Pinwheel Home phone will also integrate with Pinwheel's watches and smartphones, enabling children to use the same phone number across devices.

### Pinwheel Circle Service
The Pinwheel Circle service allows for free calls between Pinwheel Home devices. Plans start at $6.99 per month for up to five approved contacts, and unlimited calling plans are available for $9.99 per month. This service provides a safe and convenient way for kids to stay in touch with their friends and family.
