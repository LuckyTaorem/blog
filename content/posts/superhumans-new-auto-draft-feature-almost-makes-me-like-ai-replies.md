## Introduction to Superhuman's Auto-Draft Feature
Superhuman, a well-known email client, has recently introduced an auto-draft feature that aims to improve AI-generated email replies. This feature uses a mixture of models, including frontier models from both Anthropic and Open AI, to create draft responses that sound less robotic. According to Rahul Vohra, Superhuman's co-founder, "Today, we are using a mixture of models to make this work. The actual writing is done by frontier models from both Anthropic and Open AI. So we’re applying the maximum amount of intelligence and context to this that we possibly can to make the feature work."

## How Superhuman's Auto-Draft Feature Works
The auto-draft feature identifies important emails and creates draft responses based on the content of the email. This feature is designed to save users time and effort in responding to emails.
