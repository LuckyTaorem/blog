## Kyber's Technology
Kyber's core software is an SDK that enables the synchronization of video, audio, sensor data, and control inputs with minimal latency. This technology is built on the principles of video-streaming, which Kempf and his team have expertise in, thanks to their experience with VLC Media Player and cloud gaming startup Shadow. The Kyber platform is designed to optimize performance, taking into account the available compute resources of each device, and ensuring seamless communication between devices.
