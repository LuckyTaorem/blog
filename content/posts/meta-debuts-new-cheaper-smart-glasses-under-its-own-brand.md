## Introduction to Meta's New Smart Glasses
Meta, a leading technology company, has recently announced the launch of its new line of smart glasses, dubbed Meta Glasses. These glasses are the result of a partnership between Meta and Essilor Luxottica, a well-known manufacturer of eyewear. The Meta Glasses are available in several countries, starting at a price of $299, making them a more affordable option in the wearables market.

### Key Features of Meta Glasses
The Meta Glasses come with a range of features, including:
* A camera for capturing photos and videos
* Personal speakers for listening to music or taking calls
* A dedicated button that triggers the Meta AI assistant by default, but can be customized to launch a specific feature
* Over eight hours of battery life, with an on-the-go charging case providing up to 40 hours of additional usage
* Support for the "Pedestrian navigation" feature, which provides turn-by-turn directions for walking around
* Support for 14 new languages for its live translation feature, including Japanese, Chinese (Mandarin), Hindi, and Korean

## Design and Variations of Meta Glasses
The Meta Glasses are available in several designs and variations, including:
* The "Meta Adventurer" frame, which features a rectangular shape and is available in standard and large sizes
* The "Meta Fury" frame, which is boxier and resembles popular styles of men's glasses
* The "Meta Glasses by Kylie" frame, which features a slim oval frame designed in collaboration with American model Kylie Jenner
