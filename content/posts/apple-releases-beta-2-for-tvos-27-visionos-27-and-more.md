### A Legacy of Apple Journalism

- **Founding Vision**: Established in 2008, 9to5Mac began as a niche blog focused on Mac hardware repairs and software tweaks.
- **Transition to News**: By 2012, the site pivoted to real-time Apple news, leveraging the rapid release cycle of iOS, macOS, and wearables.
- **Current Reach**: Over 15 million monthly visitors, an average dwell time of 7 minutes, and a 45% bounce rate reduction thanks to deep content.
