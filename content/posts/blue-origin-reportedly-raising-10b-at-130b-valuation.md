## Blue Origin's Plans and Projects
Blue Origin has several projects in the pipeline, including the development of its flagship rocket, New Glenn. Although the company faced a major setback when New Glenn exploded during testing in late May, it plans to rebuild its launchpad in Cape Canaveral and use New Glenn for launches later this year. Blue Origin is also working on a satellite internet network using thousands of satellites, a project that could potentially rival SpaceX's Starlink. The company's involvement in NASA's Artemis missions, aimed at returning humans to the moon, further underscores its commitment to space exploration.
