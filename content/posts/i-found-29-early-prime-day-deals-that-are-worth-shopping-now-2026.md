## **Why Shop Early for Prime Day Deals?**
Prime Day is notorious for **limited stock, flash sales, and lightning deals** that disappear in minutes. Shopping early gives you a significant advantage:

- **First dibs on inventory**: The most popular items sell out fast. Early shoppers avoid the last-minute rush.

- **Avoid shipping delays**: With millions of orders flooding Amazon’s warehouses, early purchases ensure faster delivery.
- **More time to compare**: You can research alternatives without the pressure of a ticking clock.
