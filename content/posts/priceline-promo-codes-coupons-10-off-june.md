## The Evolution of Priceline: From "Name Your Own Price" to VIP Rewards

Priceline has been a cornerstone of online travel booking since its inception in 1997. The platform revolutionized the industry with its innovative "Name Your Own Price" system, which allowed travelers to bid on airline tickets, hotel rooms, and car rentals without knowing the exact details until after purchase. This opaque pricing model was both controversial and groundbreaking, setting Priceline apart from competitors like Expedia and Orbitz.

### The "Name Your Own Price" Legacy
The original system was simple yet risky:
- Travelers set their budget for a specific travel category (e.g., $100 for a hotel in New York).
- Priceline matched the bid with available inventory from partners.
- The exact hotel or flight details were revealed only after the credit card was charged.
- All sales were final, with no cancellations or refunds.

This model thrived in the early 2000s but faced criticism for its lack of transparency. Travelers often felt misled when they received a 3-star hotel instead of the 4-star they expected. Over time, Priceline phased out this system in favor of more transparent alternatives like **Express Deals**, which we’ll explore later.

### The Rise of Celebrity Endorsements
Priceline’s marketing strategy was equally innovative. The company partnered with **William Shatner** (yes, *Star Trek*’s Captain Kirk) in a series of iconic commercials where he played "The Negotiator." These ads were so memorable that Shatner became synonymous with Priceline for an entire generation. While celebrity endorsements are no longer the backbone of Priceline’s marketing, the legacy of those ads lives on in pop culture.

## How Priceline’s Discount Systems Work in 2026

Priceline has evolved far beyond its "Name Your Own Price" roots. Today, the platform offers multiple ways to save, from **VIP-exclusive discounts** to **Express Deals** and **Fan Fest Getaways**. Let’s break down each system in detail.

### 1. VIP Rewards: The Ultimate Travel Hack
Signing up for Priceline’s **VIP Rewards** is the easiest way to unlock exclusive savings. The program is free and offers:

### 2. Express Deals: Opaque Pricing 2.0
Express Deals are Priceline’s modern take on opaque pricing. Here’s how they work:
- **Discounted Rates**: Up to 60% off hotels, flights, and car rentals.
- **Limited-Time Offers**: Deals expire quickly, so act fast.
- **Hidden Details**: The exact hotel name or flight time is revealed only after booking.
- **Non-Refundable**: Like the old "Name Your Own Price" system, these deals are final.

**How to Use Express Deals:**
1. Search for hotels, flights, or car rentals on Priceline.
2. Look for the **Express Deals badge** in search results.
3. Apply a promo code (e.g., **FANFEST** for $20 off) at checkout.
4. Complete the booking to reveal the details.

**Pro Tip:** Express Deals are ideal for flexible travelers who prioritize price over brand. If you’re particular about your hotel chain or flight time, this may not be the best option.

**A:** It depends on the deal:
