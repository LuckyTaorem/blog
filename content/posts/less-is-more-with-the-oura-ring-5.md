## Introduction to the Oura Ring 5
The Oura Ring 5 is the latest iteration of the Oura Ring series, a line of smart rings designed to track various aspects of a user's health and wellness. As a smartwatch alternative, the Oura Ring 5 offers a unique approach to health tracking, focusing on simplicity and comfort. In this article, we will delve into the features and capabilities of the Oura Ring 5, comparing it to its predecessors, the Oura Ring 4 and Oura Ring Gen 3.

### What to Expect from the Oura Ring 5
The Oura Ring 5 is designed to provide users with a comprehensive overview of their health and wellness. Some of the key features of the Oura Ring 5 include:
* Advanced sleep tracking, including duration, quality, and stages of sleep
* Continuous heart rate monitoring and rhythm analysis
* Activity tracking, including steps taken, distance traveled, and calories burned
