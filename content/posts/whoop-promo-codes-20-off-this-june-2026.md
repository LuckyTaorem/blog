### What is Whoop?
Whoop is a wearable fitness tracker that offers exhaustive activity tracking and biometric data features. Whoop has been reviewed by various testers, including those from WIRED, who have praised its advanced features and accuracy.

### Features of Whoop Fitness Trackers
The Whoop fitness tracker offers a range of features, including:
* Activity tracking: Whoop tracks various physical activities, such as running, cycling, and swimming, providing users with detailed analytics and insights.
* Biometric data: The device monitors heart rate, blood oxygen levels, and other biometric data to provide users with a comprehensive picture of their physical health.
* Sleep monitoring: Whoop tracks sleep patterns, including duration, quality, and stages of sleep, to help users optimize their rest and recovery.
* Recovery monitoring: The device monitors recovery levels, providing users with personalized recommendations to improve their physical performance.
* Stress monitoring: Whoop tracks stress levels, helping users to identify and manage stressors in their daily lives.
