## Introduction to Wearable Health Trackers
Wearable health trackers have become increasingly popular in recent years, with many companies releasing their own versions of these devices. The Ring Conn 3 is one such device, a wearable health tracker that aims to provide users with a comprehensive overview of their health metrics. In this article, we will be discussing the Ring Conn 3, its features, and the experience of Amanda Silberling, a senior writer at Tech Crunch, who purchased and returned the device due to its lack of effectiveness.

## Features of the Ring Conn 3
The Ring Conn 3 is a wearable health tracker that comes with a range of features, including:
* A starting price of $349
* A thickness of 2.3 mm
* Water-resistance
* A battery life of up to 14 days on one charge
* A screen-less form factor
* Aesthetics available in rose gold and brushed metal
* A sizing kit included
* Compatibility with iOS and Android devices

## Amanda Silberling's Experience with the Ring Conn 3
Amanda Silberling, a senior writer at Tech Crunch, purchased the Ring Conn 3 but returned it after 10 days due to its lack of effectiveness in tracking health metrics. She mentioned that she was disappointed with the device's performance, particularly with its headache detection feature, which was supposed to warn her of oncoming headaches by monitoring her heart rate variability. Silberling stated, "I thought the Ring Conn would’ve been worth it if only for its headache detection feature, which supposedly warns you of oncoming headaches by monitoring your heart rate variability." However, she found that the feature did not work as expected, leading her to return the device.
