## Introduction to Google Chat
Google Chat is a feature-packed messaging app that deserves more attention. It offers a wide range of features that can help users make the most out of it. From Spaces to threaded messages, Google Chat has everything you need to stay connected with your friends, family, and colleagues.

### Key Features of Google Chat
Some of the key features of Google Chat include:
* **Spaces**: Can have names and descriptions, can be used to assign tasks to specific people, and file sharing is more elegantly handled in Spaces
* **Threaded Messages**: Available in one-to-one chats, group chats, and Spaces, can be used to spin off separate tangents within conversations
* **Pinned Messages**: Can be pinned from favorite contacts to the top of the list, available on the web and mobile apps
* **Sections**: Can be used to organize conversations in sections (like folders for emails), available on the web and mobile apps
* **Message Scheduling**: Available on the web app, can schedule messages for delivery up to 120 days in advance
* **Integration with Google Products**: Can attach links from Google Calendar, can attach files from Google Drive, can create tasks in Google Tasks from messages, and can forward messages to the linked Gmail inbox

### Access Restrictions and Status
Google Chat also allows you to set up access restrictions to stop unwanted contact. You can configure it to only allow people already in your contacts list to get in touch via the Google Chat platform. Additionally, you can set your status to let contacts know when you're around, away from your desk, or away for an extended period of time.
