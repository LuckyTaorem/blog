## Concerns Surrounding AI Data Centers
Despite the benefits of AI data centers, there are several concerns surrounding these facilities, including:
* Environmental impact: Data centers require large amounts of energy to operate, which can contribute to greenhouse gas emissions and climate change.
* Health risks: The construction and operation of data centers can pose health risks to nearby residents, including exposure to toxic chemicals and noise pollution.
* Community disruption: The construction of data centers can disrupt local communities, including the destruction of natural habitats and the displacement of residents.
