## Introduction to the Moratorium
New York state has imposed a one-year moratorium on the construction of massive new data centers, specifically those using 50 megawatts or more. This move is intended to allow the state to figure out "consistent standards" for responsible data center development in New York. The decision comes amidst growing concerns about pollution, rising energy costs, and diminishing water supplies associated with data center operations.
