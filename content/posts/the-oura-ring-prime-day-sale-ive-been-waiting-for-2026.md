### Key Features of the Oura Ring 4

The Oura Ring 4 is packed with features that set it apart from other wearables:

- **Advanced Sleep Tracking**: The Oura Ring is renowned for its **sleep analysis**, providing detailed insights into sleep stages (light, deep, REM), sleep efficiency, and disturbances. It also offers a **Sleep Score** to help users understand their sleep quality at a glance.
- **Activity and Readiness Tracking**: The ring monitors daily activity levels, including steps, calories burned, and active time. It also provides a **Readiness Score**, which assesses how prepared your body is for the day based on factors like recovery, sleep, and activity.

- **Long Battery Life**: With a battery life of **up to 7 days**, the Oura Ring outlasts many smartwatches, reducing the hassle of frequent charging.
- **Water Resistance**: The ring is **water-resistant up to 100 meters**, making it suitable for swimming and showering.

- **Recovery metrics** (HRV, resting heart rate, body temperature)

For those who prefer a **low-maintenance wearable**, the Oura Ring 4 is a fantastic alternative to smartwatches, which often require manual workout logging.
