### The Evolution of Mac Security Threats
In the past, Macs were often considered immune to viruses and malware due to their relatively small market share compared to PCs. However, as Macs have gained popularity, they have become a more attractive target for cybercriminals. Today, Macs are vulnerable to a wide range of threats, including:

* Malware: malicious software designed to harm or exploit your Mac
* Spyware: software that secretly monitors your activity and steals sensitive data
* Ransomware: malware that encrypts your files and demands payment in exchange for the decryption key
* Phishing: scams that trick you into revealing sensitive information, such as passwords or credit card numbers
