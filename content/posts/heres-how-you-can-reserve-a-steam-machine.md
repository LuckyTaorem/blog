## Reserving Your Steam Machine
To reserve your Steam Machine, follow these steps:

1. **Visit the Steam website**: Go to the official Steam website and navigate to the Steam Machine reservation page.
2. **Sign in to your Steam account**: If you're not already signed in, log in to your Steam account to access the reservation system.
3. **Select your preferred model**: Choose the Steam Machine model you want to reserve, taking into account the features and pricing options.
4. **Provide your contact information**: Enter your contact details, including your email address and phone number, to receive updates on your reservation status.
5. **Wait for confirmation**: After submitting your reservation, wait for an email confirmation from Valve, which will include further instructions on how to complete your purchase.
