The **Apple Watch Series 11** builds upon the success of its predecessors with a host of **new features, performance upgrades, and design refinements**. Here’s a breakdown of what sets it apart:

- **IP6X dust resistance** and **WR50 water resistance**, making it suitable for extreme conditions.

#### **4. Battery Life and Charging Improvements**
- **Up to 36 hours of battery life** in standard mode, with **low-power mode extending it to 72 hours**.
