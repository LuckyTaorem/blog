## Introduction to Spotify's AI Music Assistant
Spotify has recently announced the expansion of its AI push with a ChatGPT-like music assistant. This new feature allows users to chat with Spotify across the app's Home and Now Playing views on mobile devices. The assistant is designed to help users choose what to play next, explore their listening history, and discover new music.

## Key Features of the AI Music Assistant
The AI music assistant offers several key features, including:
* Users can type or speak to the app and have back-and-forth conversations to help them choose what to play next
* The app will also be able to chat with users about their listening history and can help them learn more about their favorite music or go deeper into podcasts or audiobooks
* Users can ask about their own listening history, such as when was the first time they played a certain track, or explore what sort of genres they've been streaming lately
* Users can ask Spotify to play some artists they haven't heard before, add a specific artist by name, narrow the selection to just more recent tracks, or give it other directions
* Users can also ask Spotify to save songs, add songs to their queue, or follow the artist via the new feature
