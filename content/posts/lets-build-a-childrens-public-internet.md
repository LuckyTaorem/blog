## Introduction to Online Safety for Minors
The internet has become an essential part of our daily lives, and its impact on children is a growing concern. With the rise of social media and online platforms, children are exposed to various risks, including addiction, self-esteem issues, and potential exposure to predators. Several countries have implemented age verification or bans for minors to mitigate these risks. Recently, the US House of Representatives passed the Kids Internet and Digital Safety (KIDS) Act, aiming to regulate online child safety.
