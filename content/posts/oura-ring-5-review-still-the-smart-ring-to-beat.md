## Introduction to the Oura Ring 5
The Oura Ring 5 is the latest iteration of the smart ring from Oura, a company that has been a leader in the smart ring category since 2015. With the Oura Ring 5, the company aims to continue its dominance in the market with a range of new features and improvements. In this article, we will delve into the details of the Oura Ring 5, including its design, features, and pricing.

### Design and Comfort
The Oura Ring 5 boasts a smaller design, 40% smaller than its predecessor, the Ring 4. This reduction in size makes the ring more comfortable to wear, and the titanium exterior with a tougher physical vapor deposition coating adds to its durability. The ring also has an IP68 rating and 100-meter water resistance, making it suitable for wear during various activities, including swimming and showering.

## Features of the Oura Ring 5
The Oura Ring 5 comes with a range of features that make it an attractive option for those looking for a smart ring. Some of the key features include:
* Live Activity feature, which allows users to start workouts from their phone and view pace, distance, and heart rate on lock-screen widgets
* Automatic Activity Detection, which is more reliable and can recognize walks and Pilates sessions
* Third-party heart rate monitor pairing via Bluetooth
* Sleep tracking, which consistently produces believable data and accurately reflects nights when staying up too late or waking up multiple times
* Symptom Radar, which flags subtle physiological changes about a day after realizing one is getting sick
* Health Radar, which includes Blood Pressure Signals and Nighttime Breathing
* GLP-1 tracking, which allows users to log injections or oral medications, monitor side effects, and record weight changes
