## Introduction to the Lawsuit
Apple has filed a lawsuit against Open AI in Northern California federal court, accusing the company of stealing its trade secrets. The lawsuit, which is 41 pages long, alleges that former Apple employees stole confidential information related to "product development, manufacturing, supply chain, technology research, and other innovations" for the benefit of Open AI. This is not the first lawsuit Open AI has been involved in this year, as the company has been embroiled in multiple legal battles.
