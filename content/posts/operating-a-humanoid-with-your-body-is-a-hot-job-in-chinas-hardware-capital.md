## What are Humanoid Robots?
Humanoid robots are robots designed to resemble and mimic human appearance and behavior. They are equipped with advanced artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms that enable them to learn, adapt, and interact with their environment in a human-like way. Humanoid robots have various applications in fields such as healthcare, education, and manufacturing, where they can perform tasks that require human-like dexterity and interaction.

## How Do VR Rigs Control Humanoid Robots?
The VR rigs used by workers at IO-AI Tech are designed to provide an immersive and interactive experience, allowing users to control humanoid robots with precision and accuracy. The VR rig consists of a headset, controllers, and sensors that track the user's movements and translate them into commands for the robot. This technology enables workers to control the robot's movements, gestures, and interactions in a natural and intuitive way, as if they were controlling their own body.
