Fi Ultra is a pet tracker developed by Fi, a company that specializes in creating innovative pet care solutions. Fi Ultra works by using a combination of GPS, LTE, and Starlink satellite technology to track your dog's location. The device is equipped with a GPS module that provides accurate location data, while the LTE connectivity allows for real-time tracking and updates. However, in areas with no LTE coverage, Fi Ultra automatically switches to Starlink satellite mode, ensuring that you can still track your dog's location. This feature is particularly useful for pet owners who live in rural or remote areas where LTE coverage may be limited or non-existent.
