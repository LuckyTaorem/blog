## Introduction to Google's Sustainability Efforts
Google has recently struck a deal to purchase the entire electrical output from the Steel River Energy Center solar project in Arkansas. This move is aimed at offsetting the company's fossil fuel emissions, which have been increasing due to the growing demand for its data centers. The Steel River Energy Center solar project is expected to come online in 2029 and will use US-manufactured steel and solar panels.

## The Steel River Energy Center Solar Project
The Steel River Energy Center solar project will have an initial output of 1.6 GW of solar power and 2 GW of battery storage. Upon completion, the total output will be 2.45 GW of solar power and 2.9 GWh of battery energy storage. This will be enough to power approximately 315,000 homes. Google's decision to purchase the entire output of the solar project is a significant step towards reducing its carbon footprint.
