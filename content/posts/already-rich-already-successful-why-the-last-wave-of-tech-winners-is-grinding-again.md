## Introduction to the Trend
A notable pattern is emerging in the tech industry, where individuals who have already achieved significant success are choosing to dedicate themselves to Artificial Intelligence (AI) projects. This trend is observed among co-founders of successful companies, CEOs, and other high-profile figures in the tech world. The motivation behind this move seems to be the fear of missing out on the defining moment of AI and the potential for even greater financial gains. As seen with [Anthropic's latest feud with the government](https://ltdeveloperblogs.github.io/posts/three-things-to-watch-amid-anthropics-latest-feud-with-the-government/), the AI landscape is complex and evolving rapidly.

## Key Players and Their Moves
Several key players in the tech industry are making significant moves into the AI space. Tom Blomfield, co-founder of Go Cardless and Monzo, has taken on a role as a Group Partner at Y Combinator. Mike Krieger, co-founder of Instagram, is now the chief product officer at Anthropic. Andrej Karpathy, a founding member of Open AI and leader of AI at Tesla, has founded Eureka Labs. These moves indicate a strong belief in the future of AI and its potential for growth and innovation. Chamath Palihapitiya, known as the SPAC King and CEO of 8090 Labs, has expressed his conviction in the importance of what they are building now, stating, "I am convinced that what we are building now is even more important, so there was no decision to make except to be all in."

### Financial Investments and Funding
The financial aspect of this trend is also noteworthy. 8090 Labs, an enterprise AI coding startup, has secured $135 million in Series A funding led by Salesforce Ventures. Navigate AI, an AI "copilot" for construction workers, has received $25 million in seed funding. These investments demonstrate the confidence of investors in the potential of AI to transform various industries. Additionally, the revenue of companies like Workday, which has reached $8 billion, shows the scale at which these businesses operate and the potential impact of integrating AI into their operations.

## Conclusion and Future Outlook
The trend of successful tech individuals returning to work on AI projects underscores the significance of AI in the future of technology.
