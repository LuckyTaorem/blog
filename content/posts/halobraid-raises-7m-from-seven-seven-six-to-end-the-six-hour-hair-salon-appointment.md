## Introduction to Hair Braiding and the Problem it Presents
Hair braiding is an ancient art form that has been a staple in many cultures, particularly in the African and African American communities. The process of braiding hair is not only a means of styling but also a way of expressing one's cultural identity and heritage. However, the traditional method of braiding can be time-consuming and labor-intensive, often requiring several hours to complete. This has led to the emergence of a new problem - the need for a more efficient and automated solution for hair braiding.

## The Birth of Halo Braid
Halo Braid is a robotics startup that aims to revolutionize the hair braiding industry by introducing an automated braiding device. The company was founded by Yinka Ogunbiyi, an engineer and entrepreneur who has a passion for solving complex problems. Ogunbiyi's journey to creating Halo Braid began during the COVID-19 pandemic, when she was stuck alone in her London apartment and tried to braid her own hair. The experience was frustrating and time-consuming, and it sparked an idea in her mind - what if there was a way to automate the braiding process?

### Research and Development
Ogunbiyi spent several years researching and developing the concept of an automated braiding device. She conducted surveys and gathered data on the hair braiding industry, which revealed some astonishing statistics. For example, people spend an estimated 8 billion hours braiding hair each year, and 95% of respondents said they would get their hair braided more often if it took less time. These findings solidified Ogunbiyi's conviction that there was a need for a more efficient and automated solution for hair braiding.

## The Technology Behind Halo Braid
The Halo Braid device is designed to work in conjunction with human stylists, rather than replacing them. The device is equipped with advanced robotics and artificial intelligence, which enable it to learn and adapt to different hair types and braiding styles. The process of using the device is straightforward - a stylist starts the braiding process and then hands it off to the Halo Braid device, which can finish the rest of the braid in seconds.

### Key Features of the Halo Braid Device
Some of the key features of the Halo Braid device include:
* Advanced robotics and artificial intelligence
* Ability to learn and adapt to different hair types and braiding styles
* Gentle on hair, reducing breakage and damage
* Can finish both knotless and box braids
* Compatible with a wide range of hair types and textures
