## Introduction to the Steam Machine
The Steam Machine is a long-awaited living room-friendly PC designed by Valve, a renowned company in the gaming industry. After months of anticipation, Valve has finally announced the pricing and release details of the Steam Machine. The base model will start at $1,049, and it will be available for purchase starting June 29th. In this article, we will delve into the details of the Steam Machine, its features, and what makes it an exciting addition to the gaming world.

### What is the Steam Machine?
The Steam Machine is a compact, console-like PC that is designed to bring the Steam gaming experience to the living room. It is a hardware device that runs on the SteamOS operating system, which is a Linux-based platform developed by Valve. The Steam Machine is designed to be a competitor to traditional gaming consoles like the PlayStation and Xbox, but with the added benefit of being a full-fledged PC.

### Key Features of the Steam Machine
Some of the key features of the Steam Machine include:

* Support for a wide range of Steam games, including popular titles and indie games
* The ability to stream games from a PC to the Steam Machine using Steam's in-home streaming feature
