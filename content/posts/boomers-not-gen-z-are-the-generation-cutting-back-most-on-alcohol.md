## Introduction to the Trend
The latest data from market researcher IWSR indicates a significant shift in alcohol consumption patterns across different generations. Contrary to popular belief, it's not Gen Z that's cutting back most on alcohol, but rather the baby boomer generation. This trend towards moderation is observed at every level of society, with boomers having the lowest drinking rate of any generation.

## Understanding the Data
The statistics show that 71% of boomers consumed alcohol in the past six months, which marks a 2 percentage point decrease from three years ago. In contrast, 74% of Gen Z reported drinking in the past six months, up from 66% three years ago. The total adult population drinking rate stands at 76%. These numbers suggest that while Gen Z is often associated with health-conscious and mindful living, it's actually the older generation that's embracing moderation more wholeheartedly.
