## Introduction to AI Regulation
The rapid development of artificial intelligence (AI) has raised concerns about its potential impact on society. Demis Hassabis, CEO and co-founder of Google DeepMind, has suggested the need for a global AI watchdog to regulate the development and deployment of AI systems. This proposal has sparked a discussion about the role of regulation in ensuring the safe and responsible development of AI.

## The Proposed AI Watchdog
The proposed AI watchdog would be led by the US and would consist of leading independent experts and representatives from open-source communities. The watchdog would have the authority to evaluate frontier models before they are released and coordinate an appropriate response if necessary. This would help to prevent the misuse of AI and ensure that its development is aligned with human values.

### Key Features of the Watchdog
Some key features of the proposed watchdog include:
* Evaluation of frontier models before release
* Coordination of responses to potential AI risks
* Representation from open-source communities
* Leadership by the US

The proposed watchdog would be similar to existing regulators like the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority.
