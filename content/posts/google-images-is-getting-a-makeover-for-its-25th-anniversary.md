## Introduction to Google Images Makeover
Google Images is getting a makeover for its 25th anniversary. The new version of Google Images will have "updated in real time" and "intelligently tailored to your unique interests" results. This makeover is a significant update to the search engine, which was inspired by the demand for images of Jennifer Lopez's Versace dress.

## Key Features of the New Google Images
The new Google Images interface will have several key features, including:
* Updated Google Images interface with real-time results
* Intelligent results tailored to individual interests
* Ability to save images to collections
* Saved page interface with tabs for collections and image results
* Capability to create images with a text prompt in search
* Integration of Nano Banana AI image generator in AI Overviews

As noted by Google, "People didn't just want to read about the dress — they wanted to see it." This quote highlights the importance of visual search and the need for a more advanced image search engine.
