GPT-Live-1 is a new voice model designed to enhance ChatGPT's voice mode capabilities. According to Kundan Kumar, Open AI researcher lead, GPT-Live-1 is the "smartest voice model" that can interrupt less, wait for the user to continue speaking, and automatically pass queries to the best text models (like GPT-5.5) for research and web search.
