## Background of the Dispute
The Apple-Epic dispute began when Epic, the developer of the popular game Fortnite, introduced a direct payment system in the game, bypassing Apple's App Store payment system. Apple removed Fortnite from the App Store, citing a violation of its terms and conditions. Epic then filed a lawsuit against Apple, alleging that the company's App Store policies are anti-competitive.
