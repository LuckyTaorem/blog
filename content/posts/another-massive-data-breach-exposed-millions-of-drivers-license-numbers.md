## Introduction to the Massive Data Breach
The recent data breach at Assurance America, a company providing car and rental insurance to customers across more than a dozen U.S. states, has exposed 6.9 million driver's license numbers. This breach is considered one of the largest known spills of Americans' driver's license information this year. The incident occurred on March 17, and the investigation concluded on June 15. In this article, we will delve into the details of the breach, its implications, and the measures that can be taken to prevent such incidents in the future.

### How the Breach Occurred
According to the reports, the breach occurred when hackers targeted one of Assurance America's employees. The company promptly disabled the compromised credentials, but not before the hackers had accessed the sensitive information.
