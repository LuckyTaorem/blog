## How to Choose the Right Robot Vacuum  
When comparing models, don’t just chase the biggest discount. Consider:  
- **Floor type:** Homes with thick carpets benefit from stronger suction (2,500 Pa or higher), while hardwood floors pair well with hybrid mop‑vac units.  
- **Smart features:** Lidar navigation and AI object recognition save time and frustration, especially in cluttered households.  
- **Maintenance:** Self‑emptying bases reduce hands‑on upkeep, but they add cost. Decide if convenience outweighs budget.  
- **App ecosystem:** Integration with Alexa, Google Assistant, or Apple HomeKit can make scheduling and control seamless.

## Tips to Maximize Your Prime Day Savings  
1. **Set Alerts Early:** Use price‑tracking tools like CamelCamelCamel or Keepa to confirm whether a “deal” is truly a discount.  
2. **Bundle Smartly:** Some retailers offer bundles with extra filters, mop pads, or extended warranties — often better value than standalone units.
